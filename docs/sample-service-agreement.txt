SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is entered into by Northstar Studio ("Client") and Jordan Lee
("Contractor"). Contractor will provide website design and development services.

Scope of Work and Deliverables. Contractor will deliver a responsive five-page website,
design files, and deployment documentation by September 30. Client will provide feedback
within five business days of each milestone.

Payment. Client will pay a total fee of $8,000. Fifty percent is due at signing and the
remaining balance is due within 15 days after final delivery.

Confidentiality. Each party will protect confidential information received from the
other party and will use it only to perform this agreement.

Intellectual Property. After full payment, Client owns the final deliverables. Contractor
retains ownership of pre-existing tools, templates, and know-how.

Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice.
Client will pay for work completed through the termination date.

Limitation of Liability. Each party's total liability is limited to the fees paid under
this agreement.

Dispute Resolution and Governing Law. The parties will first attempt good-faith
mediation. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.
