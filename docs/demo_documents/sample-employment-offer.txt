SYNTHETIC DEMO DOCUMENT - EMPLOYMENT OFFER LETTER

This Employment Offer Letter is issued by Meridian Robotics Private Limited, a
fictional robotics startup in Hyderabad, India ("Company"), to Arjun Rao, a
fictional candidate residing in Chennai, India ("Employee"). The Company offers
Employee the position of Product Operations Associate.

1. Role and Start Date
Employee will join as Product Operations Associate on August 1, 2026. Employee's
responsibilities include vendor coordination, product data review, operations
reporting, customer issue tracking, and other duties assigned by the Company.

2. Compensation
Employee will receive a fixed annual salary of INR 8,40,000, payable monthly
subject to tax deductions. Employee may be eligible for a discretionary annual
bonus based on Company performance, manager review, and leadership approval.

3. Working Hours and Location
Employee will work from the Hyderabad office or remotely as approved by the
Company. Normal working hours are 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday through Friday,
with flexibility required during product launches or urgent customer escalations.

4. Confidentiality
Employee must keep Company source materials, customer information, vendor
pricing, product plans, internal dashboards, and financial data confidential
during and after employment.

5. Intellectual Property
All work product, documents, inventions, workflows, designs, reports, product
ideas, and process improvements created by Employee during employment and related
to Company business will belong to the Company.

6. Probation and Termination
Employee will be on probation for six months. During probation, either party may
terminate employment with seven days notice. After probation, either party may
terminate employment with thirty days notice or salary in lieu of notice.

7. Policies
Employee must comply with Company policies, including information security,
expense, leave, workplace conduct, and acceptable use policies, as updated from
time to time.

8. Jurisdiction
This offer and employment relationship are governed by the laws of India. Courts
in Hyderabad, Telangana will have jurisdiction.

9. Risky or Ambiguous Terms for Demo Review
"Other duties assigned by the Company" is broad and could expand the role without
limits. The discretionary bonus has no measurable criteria. The IP clause may be
too broad because it does not exclude Employee's prior personal projects. The
offer references policies that may change but does not attach or summarize them.
