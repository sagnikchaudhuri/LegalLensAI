SYNTHETIC DEMO DOCUMENT - MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into by Aurora Analytics Private
Limited, a fictional company incorporated in Bengaluru, India ("Aurora"), and
Northbridge Design Labs, a fictional product design studio based in Pune, India
("Northbridge"). The parties wish to discuss a possible collaboration involving
workflow automation prototypes, customer research, technical designs, product
roadmaps, pricing models, and business plans.

1. Confidential Information
Confidential Information means non-public technical, commercial, financial,
customer, product, design, or strategic information disclosed by either party in
writing, orally, visually, or through access to systems. Confidential Information
includes prototypes, architecture diagrams, source snippets, pitch materials,
market assumptions, and notes prepared from disclosed information.

2. Purpose
The receiving party may use Confidential Information only to evaluate and discuss
the proposed collaboration between Aurora and Northbridge. The receiving party
may share Confidential Information only with employees and advisors who need to
know it for the Purpose and who are bound by confidentiality duties.

3. Obligations
The receiving party will protect Confidential Information using at least the same
care it uses for its own confidential information, and no less than reasonable
care. The receiving party will not copy, reverse engineer, publish, sell, license,
or disclose Confidential Information except as allowed by this Agreement.

4. Exclusions
Confidential Information does not include information that is publicly available
without breach, already known by the receiving party before disclosure, received
lawfully from a third party, or independently developed without use of the
disclosing party's Confidential Information.

5. Term and Termination
This Agreement begins on the last signature date and continues for one year.
Either party may terminate discussions at any time by written notice. The
confidentiality obligations survive for two years after termination.

6. Return or Deletion
Upon written request, the receiving party will return or delete Confidential
Information within thirty days, except one archival copy may be retained for
legal compliance.

7. Intellectual Property
All Confidential Information remains the property of the disclosing party. No
license or transfer of intellectual property is granted except the limited right
to use Confidential Information for the Purpose.

8. Remedies
Unauthorized disclosure may cause irreparable harm. The disclosing party may seek
injunctive relief in addition to other available remedies.

9. Jurisdiction
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Courts located in Bengaluru,
Karnataka will have exclusive jurisdiction.

10. Risky or Ambiguous Terms for Demo Review
The Agreement does not define security controls for cloud storage or AI tools.
The Agreement does not clearly state whether confidentiality survives for trade
secrets beyond two years. The Agreement does not include a detailed breach notice
timeline. The Agreement allows advisors to access Confidential Information but
does not require written approval before sharing with them.
