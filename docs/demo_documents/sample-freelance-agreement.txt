SYNTHETIC DEMO DOCUMENT - FREELANCE AGREEMENT

This Freelance Agreement is made between Cedar Grove Media LLP, a fictional
marketing agency in Mumbai, India ("Client"), and Riya Mehta, a fictional
independent content strategist in Jaipur, India ("Freelancer"). Client engages
Freelancer to provide campaign strategy, copywriting, and brand messaging
services for a product launch.

1. Services
Freelancer will prepare a messaging strategy, landing page copy, ten social media
captions, three email drafts, and one campaign summary document. Client will
provide source materials, brand guidelines, and feedback within five business
days of receiving each draft.

2. Payment
Client will pay Freelancer INR 95,000 for the project. INR 35,000 is due on
signing, INR 30,000 is due after first draft delivery, and INR 30,000 is due
after final handover. Late payments may be delayed without penalty if Client is
waiting for internal approvals.

3. Timeline
Freelancer will use reasonable efforts to complete the services within six weeks
after receiving all required materials from Client.

4. Revisions
Two rounds of revisions are included. Additional revisions may be charged at INR
3,000 per hour if agreed in writing.

5. Intellectual Property
After full payment, Client owns the final approved deliverables. Freelancer owns
pre-existing templates, tools, style guides, research methods, and portfolio
knowledge. Freelancer may display excerpts of the final work in a portfolio
unless Client objects in writing.

6. Confidentiality
Freelancer will keep Client campaign plans, unpublished product details, customer
lists, and pricing strategy confidential. Client will keep Freelancer's pricing,
methods, and unpublished samples confidential.

7. Termination
Either party may terminate with seven days written notice. If Client terminates
for convenience, Client will pay for work completed up to the termination date.
If Freelancer terminates, Freelancer will refund any unused advance amount.

8. Independent Contractor
Freelancer is an independent contractor and is responsible for taxes, equipment,
and work methods. Nothing in this Agreement creates employment, partnership, or
agency.

9. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Courts located in Mumbai,
Maharashtra will have jurisdiction.

10. Risky or Ambiguous Terms for Demo Review
The late payment clause lets Client delay payment for internal approvals without
a clear deadline. The project timeline depends on "all required materials" but
does not list what materials are required. Portfolio use may expose confidential
campaign details if Client does not object in time. There is no clear acceptance
process for final deliverables or dispute process for rejected work.
