SYNTHETIC DEMO DOCUMENT - SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is entered into by BluePeak Retail Solutions Private
Limited, a fictional retail technology company in Delhi, India ("Client"), and
HelioWorks Consulting, a fictional software consulting firm in Kochi, India
("Service Provider"). Service Provider will configure analytics dashboards and
workflow automations for Client's internal operations team.

1. Scope of Services
Service Provider will deliver three analytics dashboards, two workflow automation
scripts, basic administrator training, and deployment documentation. Services do
not include custom mobile application development, paid third-party software
licenses, or ongoing infrastructure management unless agreed separately.

2. Client Responsibilities
Client will provide access to sample data, business requirements, system
credentials, and a project contact person. Client will review deliverables within
five business days of submission.

3. Fees and Payment
Client will pay INR 4,80,000 plus applicable taxes. Forty percent is due at
signing, thirty percent after dashboard delivery, and thirty percent after final
deployment. Expenses above INR 10,000 require prior written approval.

4. Change Requests
Any material change to scope, timeline, integrations, or deliverables must be
agreed in writing. If the parties cannot agree on pricing for a change request,
Service Provider may proceed using reasonable commercial judgment.

5. Confidentiality and Data Handling
Each party will protect confidential business and technical information. Service
Provider will use Client data only to perform the services and will not sell or
disclose Client data to third parties except subcontractors involved in delivery.

6. Intellectual Property
Client owns final dashboards and documentation after full payment. Service
Provider retains ownership of reusable scripts, libraries, templates, know-how,
and generic automation methods.

7. Warranty
Service Provider warrants that services will be performed in a professional and
workmanlike manner for thirty days after final delivery. Client's exclusive
remedy is correction of non-conforming services.

8. Termination
Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach is not cured within
fifteen days after written notice. Client may terminate for convenience with ten
days notice and must pay for completed work and committed expenses.

9. Limitation of Liability
Neither party will be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential
damages. Total liability is capped at fees paid in the three months before the
claim.

10. Jurisdiction
This Agreement is governed by the laws of India. Courts in New Delhi will have
exclusive jurisdiction.

11. Risky or Ambiguous Terms for Demo Review
The subcontractor data-sharing language does not require Client approval or
minimum security controls. The change request clause lets Service Provider
proceed using "reasonable commercial judgment" without agreed pricing. The
liability cap may be too low for data handling or confidentiality breaches. The
Agreement does not include a clear data deletion timeline after project closure.
